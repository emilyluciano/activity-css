Mercedes- Benz Fashion Week 2014

Designers and industry gear up for much anticipated season as event extends across New York City. The new offerings and redesign have enthused returning designers to reconfigure their show production and have also attracted new designers to debut their Collections at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week.

Fashion Week in New York!

The New Year is just a few days in and already one of the most anticipated events of the year is on the horizon. The Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Fall 2014 Collections have been much talked about over the last few weeks following the announcement of exciting changes for the upcoming shows.

New York Fashion Week is kind of a big deal. Known to fashion insiders as Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week, the Spring 2014 Collections take place from September 5th-September 12th this year. That leaves a little over two weeks until the fashion frenzy begins.
